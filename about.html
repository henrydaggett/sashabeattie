Hello! I’m Sasha Mei-Li Beattie. I studied at Central Saint Martins where I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Fashion Communication & Promotion. I am an image maker - whether that means graphic design, art direction, illustration or all of the above! However, my focus is brand and digital design.

As far as hobbies go I enjoy reading fiction (nice covers are a plus), am a moderate gamer, a decent snowboarder and an occasional unicyclist (it’s a long story) with a great appreciation for food, real and fake.

I often get asked, “Where are you from?” The short answer is Hawaii, Alaska and England but I’m Scottish and Taiwanese… Even I don’t get it sometimes, so I wrote a book about it called Hāpā, which investigates concepts of identity and belonging in relation to being of mixed ethnic heritage - check it out here.
