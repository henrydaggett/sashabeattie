Tutor the Nation

Tutor the Nation is a charity that links tutors from universities across the United Kingdom to students in need, with the long-term goal of bridging the gap in education by providing less privileged student free tutorage.


Client: Tutor the Nation

Branding & Digital: Sasha Beattie

Logo

Bridging the gap in education is the main focus of Tutor the Nation and was a clear starting point for the logo - the final concept took the idea of a duo-purpose bridge, formed from the H, that is a bridge and a smile.

From this the slogan, “Bridging the gap in education with a smile” came to mind, communicating the positivity of the founders and their kind and approachable nature. It was important that the branding match this sentiment and “Bridget” quickly became TtN’s mascot and inspired a set of stickers that are sprinkled across the website and on merchandise/branding to tie together the young, friendly feeling of the brand.

Secondary Marks - “Bridget”

Bridget is the secondary logo mark for the brand - constructed using the H/smile and cutting the O in half to make her eyes, she comes in two forms, colour block and logo-outline.

Primary Colour Palette

Bold bright, almost primary colours create the base of the colour palette contrasted by full sections of black and white - the colour palette is exciting, youthful and engaging.

Stickers!

These hand drawn stickers are scattered throughout the website symbolise features of Tutor the Nation, from safeguarding to confidence and of course we have sticker Bridget.
